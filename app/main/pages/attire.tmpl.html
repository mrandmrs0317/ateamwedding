The dress code for this event is Formal with the option of Black-tie. It is an elegant evening affair, so please dress accordingly. March weather in Florida is rather nice and can sometimes have a slight chill. We are hoping for some nice weather in the 70's by the time we begin at 4pm. But living in Florida, you never reeeally know.

Ladies

  • Any form of high heels, kitty kat heels, and even dressy flats are encouraged. Please no flip-flops or sneakers.

  • Long dresses or formal cocktail dresses with dark or spring colors (i.e those featured on the website)

  • Be aware that the ceremony is on the Grande Lawn when selecting your shoes. Luckily, however, the cocktail hour by the Reflection Pool and Ballroom reception will be high-heel/stiletto-friendly.

Gentlemen

  • Suit and tie are required; dark or light colors welcomed. Tuxedo is optional.

  • Dress shoes or boots (i.e. patent leather or even some nice loafers). No flip-flops, sneakers or boat-shoes please.
